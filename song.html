Conceited By Sza

Meaning Behind SOS

There are three reason by she named the album "SOS". First, SOS means "Save Our Soul" or "Save our ship". Secondly, according to Supreme Alphabet, letter "S" stands for Self and Savior. Lastly, her friends call her by her nickname,"Sous", a shortening of her first name,Solana.
